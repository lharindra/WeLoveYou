Primary School Kits for Kids

June 18, 2016 44 83

We had started distribution school kits from day one of our journey(in fact it's the main motto of our organization). A couple of days before the event will work with school management for a detailed report of the school requirement. According to information provided by the school management, will purchase the kits(class wise) for kids. We make sure the books and materials which we are distributing are good in terms of quality and sufficient in quantity.

Segregating and Distribution is the most important segment of the event. We all set together and segregate the books and materials in the proper order to eliminate confusion at the time of distribution. We had planned to distribute a whole set of books(single rule(2), double rule(2), plain notes(4), one-side plain(4)) and materials(like pens(4(blue ink),4(black ink),a set-of(scale,pencil's,eraser,sharpener),container box and examination pad) to each kid.

Later will distribute sorted sets to all kids and few Special awards to kids who showed some awesome skills like dancing,singing and extra-cultural activities. Will also distribute bags and shops to all kids and will call off for the day.
