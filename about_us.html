OUR STORY

“We Love You” is a Non-profit organization in Hyderabad trying to extent support to students of lower sections of society by donating education material rather than giving money. We believe that wisdom is the key for any innovation. we are trying to bring change in younger generation by transforming the way they understand the term ‘education’. We do this by organizing a full day event at Govt Schools in Hyderabad starting with fun activities, providing them a platform to prove and express themselves and also by supporting them with the study material needed.

“We Love You” is started in 2013 by a group of Eng. students in the hope to bring change wherever possible. “We Love You” is still ran by the same group of students with the help of many other volunteers in various fields of Engineering, medicine and other job markets. Our Organization gets funds every month and we make sure these are spent wisely and our associates get time to time information about the usage, thus maintaining transparency and trust. We look forward to associate with you and bring some change with your support.
